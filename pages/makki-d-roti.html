MAKKI-DI-ROTI & SARSON-DA-SAAG

Ingredients:-

1. 1 Bunch palak
2. 1 Green Chilli
3. 1 Teaspoon Ginger (finely chopped)
4. 1 Teaspoon Turmeric Powder
5. 1 Medium Onion
6. 0.25 Teaspoon Hing
7. 1 Cup Water
8. 3 BUnch Fresho-Methi
9. 1 tbsp Garlic Chopped
10. Salt for taste
11. 2 tbsp Maize flour
12. 1 Teaspoon Jeera
13. 2 Teaspoon Fresho Organic-Cow Ghee
14. 1 tbsp Oil

Steps:-

For Sarson ka saag
1 : Clean, wash and cut the sarson and palak roughly.
2 : Heat oil in a cooker. Add jeera, onion, ginger, garlic and green chilly. Fry till the onion changes its colour.
3 : Now add the sarson, palak, salt, turmeric and water. Cover the lid and pressure cook for 4 whistles.
4 : Once it cools down, grind the saag coarsely in a blender.
5 : Add makki ka atta to the saag and cook on slow flame for 15 to 20 minutes.
6 : Just before serving, heat ghee and add hing and red chilli to it. Mix it with the saag.
7 : Serve hot with makkiki roti.

For Makki di roti
1 : Make a soft dough with all the ingredients except oil. Make small balls and roll each one into a thick a chapatti.
2 : Cook it on a flat tawa using oil on both sides.
