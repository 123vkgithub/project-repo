Step1:Heat 1/2 teaspoon oil – add 2 tablespoon coriander seeds,
4 red chilies, 1 teaspoon pepper, 1 teaspoon fennel seeds. Roast until golden.
Step2:Add 1/2 teaspoon oil – add 20 small onion along with 2 tomatoes.
Step3:Saute until raw smell leaves and tomatoes shrink.
Step4:Transfer to the same bowl and set aside.
Step5:Grind it to a semi fine paste along with water, Set it aside.
Step6:Rinse the pan then to the same pan – add 2 tablespoon oil heat it. Add one 1 inch cinnamon, 1 clove, 1 cardamom, a small piece stone flower,
1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds let it splutter then add 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds let it splutter. Add few curry leaves let it splutter then give a quick saute.
