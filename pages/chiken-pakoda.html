Step1:Marinate the chicken with ginger-garlic paste.
Step2:Add the remaining ingredients.
Step3:Keep aside for at least an hour.
Step4:Mix together all the ingredients for the batter and beat thoroughly.
Step5:Heat oil in a kadahi.
Step6:Dip a marinated chicken piece in the batter and deep-fry until crisp and golden brown.
Step7:Repeat with the remaining chicken pieces, draining excess oil on absorbent kitchen paper.
Step8:Serve with mint chutney.
