MATAR-PANEER

Ingredients:-

1. 200 grams paneer (cottage cheese).
2. 1 cup green peas (matar).
3. 2 medium-sized onions, finely chopped.
4. 2 medium-sized tomatoes, finely chopped.
5. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste.
6. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
7. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder.
8. 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder.
9. 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder.
10. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder.
11. 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves).
12. 1/2 cup milk.
13. 2 tablespoons oil.
14. Salt, to taste.
15. Coriander leaves, for garnishing.

Steps:-

1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions. Sauté until they turn golden brown.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and garam masala powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
5. Add green peas and cook for 5-7 minutes.
6. Add paneer cubes and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
7. Add milk and kasuri methi. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
