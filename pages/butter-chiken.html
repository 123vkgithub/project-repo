Step1:Take the boneless chicken and cut it into small pieces.
Add yogurt, red chilli powder, salt, dry fenugreek leaves, lemon juice and ginger-garlic paste.
Mix it well and keep it in a fridge for marinating for at least 6 hours or overnight.
Step2:Heat the oil in a kadhai or wok and deep fry the chicken pieces till light
golden in color or till chicken is completely cooked as shown below in the picture.
Step3:Take it out from the oil and keep it aside.
Heat the oil in another pan or wok and add chopped onion, cumin seeds and green chilli.
Fry it for about 4-5 minutes or till onion is mushy.
Step4:Add chopped tomato, Cashew nuts, almonds, red chilli powder, coriander powder, dry fenugreek leaves, bay leaves and salt.
Cook the spices for another 2-3 minutes.
Now let it completely cool by itself.
