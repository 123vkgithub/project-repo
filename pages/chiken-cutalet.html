Step1:In a nonstick skillet, heat the oil.
Step2:Sauté the garlic for a minute..
Step3:Mix in the onion and cook until it is transparent..
Step4:Add in the ginger-garlic paste and sauté well..
Step5:Mix in the salt, chilli powder, crushed peppercorns and turmeric powder..
Step6:Cook until the chicken is thoroughly cooked, stirring occasionally..
Step7:Stir in the coriander leaves..
Step8:Place in another bowl and let the mixture cool down.
Step9:Mix in the grated potatoes well..
Step10:Here once again, add the breadcrumbs.
Step11:Make equal quantities of the mixture and form them into cutlets..
