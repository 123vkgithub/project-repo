Step1: Whisk the batter for a thin paste and then pour all of it in a pan.

Step2:Cook from one side and for the other side take another non-stick pan, flip the omelette on it and then cook it again.

Step3:You may add some butter or spray some oil on the other pan if you wish.

Step4:Cook the spices and onion-tomato paste until the oil/masala starts to leave the pan.

Step5:Cut the omelette into pieces and then carefully add it in the gravy.
