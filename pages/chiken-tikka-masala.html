Step1:Mix the marinate ingredients together. Now add in chopped chicken pieces and mix well. Step2:Let this marinate in the fridge over night.
Step3:Preheat the oven to the highest temperature. Mine went to 250°C(500°F).
Step4:Take the chicken out of the fridge before 30 mins so that it comes to room temperature and cooks evenly.
Step5:Arrange it in a foil lined baking tray.
Step6:Place the tray in the top rack and grill the chicken for 10 mins.
Step7:When 10 mins is over take the chicken out and apply the rest of the marinate over this and place it back in the oven for 10 mins.
Step8:Now mix lemon juice, oil, chat masala powder together for basing the chicken.
Step9:Now take the tray out.
Turn the chicken over and base it with the lemon liquid and place it back in the oven for 5 more mins.
