VEG-DISHES :-

Misal-Pav

Misal Pav is a popular Maharashtrian street food that consists of usal (a spicy curry usually made from moth beans) topped with onions, tomatoes, farsan (fried savory mixture), lemon juice, and coriander leaves.

Makki di Roti and ...

Makki-di-roti is a traditional Punjabi flatbread made from maize flour. Sarson da saag is a puree made from mustard greens, while makki di roti is a flatbread made from cornmeal It is a popular winter delicacy in North India.

Dhokla

Dhokla is a popular Gujarati snack that can be enjoyed any time of the day. It is made with gram flour or chickpea flour. There are many variations of dhokla, including khaman dhokla, rava dhokla, and sandwich dhokla.

Gobi Matar

Gobi matar is a popular North Indian dish. The dish is lightly spiced and can be garnished with mint or coriander leaves There are variations of gobi matar, including Amritsari gobi matar, which is a creamy cauliflower and peas curry.

Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji is a popular Indian street food originating from Mumbai. It is a hearty, flavorful meal of mashed vegetable gravy with soft buttery. There are variations of pav bhaji, including quick Instant Pot recipes.

Papaya-Khar

Papaya khar is a traditional Assamese dish. It is made with unripe papayas cooked with chillies and panch phoran. The dish is considered to be a digestive aid and is usually consumed at the beginning of a meal.

Matar Paneer

Matar paneer is a popular North Indian dish. It is made with paneer and green peas cooked in a spicy onion tomato masala. The dish is lightly spiced and can be garnished with mint or coriander leaves.

Paneer Jalfrezi

Paneer jalfrezi is a popular North Indian dish. The dish is lightly spiced and can be garnished with mint or coriander leaves. It is made with paneer and vegetables cooked in a spicy onion tomato masala.

Creamy Veg Korma

Creamy vegetable korma is a popular Indian dish that is typically served as a side dish. It is made with mixed vegetables cooked in a spicy onion tomato masala and flavored with coconut milk, cashews, and spices.

Dal Tadka

Dal tadka is a popular Indian dish. It is made with pigeon pea lentils (tuvar dal), onions, tomatoes, herbs and spices having a smoky charcoal flavor. The dish is served with steamed basmati rice or relished with roti or naan.

Tomato Cucumber ...

Tomato-cucumber raita is a popular Indian side dish that is typically served with biryanis, pulaos, and parathas. It is made with yogurt, tomato, cucumber, cumin, and fresh chopped coriander. It is used as a salad.

Cilantro Mint Chut...

Cilantro-mint chutney is a popular Indian condiment that is typically served with appetizers, snacks, and main courses. It is made with fresh cilantro, mint leaves, green chilies, ginger, garlic, lemon juice, and yogurt.

Paneer Butter Mas...

Paneer butter masala is a popular North Indian dish that is typically served as a side dish. It is made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and is cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based gravy. The dish is lightly spiced.

Bhindi Masala

Bhindi masala is a popular North Indian dish. It is made with okra (ladyfinger) and is cooked in a spicy onion tomato masala. The dish is lightly spiced and can be garnished with coriander leaves.

Dal Makhani

Dal makhani is a popular North Indian dish that is typically served as a side dish. It is made with whole black lentils (known as urad dal) and kidney beans (known as rajma) cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based gravy.
