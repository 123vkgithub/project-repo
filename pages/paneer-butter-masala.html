PANEER-BUTTER-MASALA

Ingredients:-

1. 250 grams paneer
2. 2 tablespoons butter
3. 1 tablespoon oil
4. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
5. 1 medium onion, finely chopped
6. 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
7. 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
8. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
9. 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
10. 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
11. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
12. 1/4 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
13. 1/4 cup cream
14. Salt to taste
15. 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves

Steps:-

1. Heat 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan.
2. Add 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add 1 finely chopped onion and saute until it turns golden brown.
4. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and saute for a minute.
5. Add 2 finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
6. Add 1/2 teaspoon of red chili powder, 1/4 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1/2 teaspoon of coriander powder, and 1/2 teaspoon of garam masala powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
7. Add 1/4 teaspoon of kasuri methi and mix well.
8. Add 1/4 cup of cream and mix well.
9. Add 250 grams of paneer and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
10. Garnish with 1 tablespoon of chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
