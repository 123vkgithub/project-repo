Step1: The first step is to properly clean the fish. In our case, we buy already cleaned slices, so we only have to rinse them well before we use them.
Either way, ensure to pat down the moisture before you start applying the spices.
Step2:Then in a small bowl, mix together salt, turmeric and hing powder.
Step3:Apply this mix to the fish pieces, ensuring to apply it evenly all over,
and set the pieces aside for 15-20 minutes.
Step3:Next, in another bowl, mix together the spices – chilli powder, garam masala powder,
coriander powder, cumin powder, garlic powder & dried coriander flakes (optional).
Step4:Apply this spice mix to the pieces of fish, ensuring to apply it evenly all over,
and set the fish aside for 30 minutes, before you start to fry it.
Step5:Once the fish has got a chance to absorb the spice rub,
it’s time to apply the crumb coating,
which is basically a mix of rava and rice flour (I also add a pinch of salt).
Step6:Place the fish pieces in this crumb coating, ensuring to coat the pieces evenly and well.
Step7:Then heat up the gas, add a few tablespoons of oil, and pan fry the fish on medium heat.
