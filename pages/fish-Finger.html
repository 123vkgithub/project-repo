Step 1 Roast breadcrumbs
Preheat the oven at 180 degree Celsius.
Spread breadcrumbs on a baking tray with cooking oil sprayed on it.
Roast them until golden for 3-4 minutes.
Transfer these golden breadcrumbs into a bowl and add parmesan cheese, salt and black pepper to taste.
Mix well and keep aside.
Step 2 Prepare the batter for coating
Increase the oven temperature to 220 degrees Celsius.
Next, spray another baking tray with cooking oil.
Then, take a bowl and mix egg, mayonnaise, all-purpose flour and, salt and pepper to prepare the batter.
Step 3 Coat fish in batter and bake
Cut the fish fillets into strips of 1.5 x 11 cm.
Dip the fillets into the batter and pick it up with tongs and toss them in the breadcrumbs to coat properly.
Transfer to the greased baking tray and spray with a little refined oil.
Place the tray in the preheated pan and bake for 12 minutes or until crispy on the outside.
Step 4 Serve fish fingers
Serve the fish fingers with tartare sauce and
garnish with lemon wedges and chopped parsley.
